Sheldon Cooper Would Love This.

This app wants to make decision making simple. Imagine what you could do with your time if you outsourced all your trivial decisions!

Can’t decide what to eat? Just type in your options and decido.

Fighting over who should pay the bill at a party? Just decido.

Can’t decide who to date? Nope. Don’t Decido. Make a pros & cons list.

Our version 1 covers trivial decision making. Our version 2 gets smarter! You’ll be able to make important decisions like who to vote, what to order on swiggy and more.
